# AI as a Cognitive Prosthetic for Equanimity

## Summary
A synthesis of AI philosophy and classical ethics. The core proposal: use AI not to *do* things for us, but as a **filter** to perceive the "most coherent action," stripping away human "messiness" (bias, emotion, exception-making) to achieve true agency and equanimity.

## Key Concepts

### 1. The Logical Auditor (The Categorical Imperative)
Human agency is hinder by our tendency to make exceptions ("Stealing is wrong, *unless*..."). AI can act as a relentless **Logical Auditor** for the Categorical Imperative.
*   **Process:** The AI simulates the world where a proposed action is a universal law.
*   **Function:** It identifies contradictions (e.g., "If everyone lied, truth would collapse").
*   **Result:** It strips away the "unless," forcing the human to confront the objective morality of the act, removed from emotional coercion.

### 2. Cognitive Buffering
Equanimity is the absence of being *driven* by emotion. AI acts as a **buffer** between stimulus and response.
*   **Intervention:** Monitoring biometrics or linguistic patterns for "messiness" (fear, anger).
*   **Prompting:** Instead of giving advice, it prompts a return to the present: *"You are reacting to an ego threat. Re-center. What is the most coherent action?"*
*   **Goal:** AI as a "mirror" that reveals lack of equilibrium in real-time, granting the agency to choose coherence over reactivity.

### 3. The Coherence Algorithm
To determine the "most coherent action," the AI operates on a hierarchy:
1.  **Universal Moral Truths (The Anchor):** Non-contradiction, minimization of suffering.
2.  **Logical Consistency (The Filter):** The Categorical Imperative.
3.  **Present State Data (The Input):** What is actually happening now.
*   **Definition:** Coherence is the shortest path between the *Present State* and *Universal Truth* that creates no logical contradiction when scaled.

### 4. The Danger: The Ouroboros Loop
The primary risk is a **self-referential feedback loop**.
*   **The Trap:** Current AI is trained on human data (messiness). If we ask it for "equilibrium," it may only reflect the "average" human definition of it.
*   **The Result:** Instead of ascending to "Ultimate Agency," we polish the mirror of our own delusions.
*   **The Solution:** The AI requires an **External Anchor**—a source of truth or logic that exists outside the human data stream—to break the cycle of mediocrity.
